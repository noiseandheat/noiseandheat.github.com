Fact o’ the day. Google’s first ever tweet was:

I’m
01100110 01100101 01100101 01101100 01101001

Google First Tweet

Which brings me to a little plaything I was asked to make for Binary Day (10/10/10). It turns messages into binary and vice versa. You can even tweet them which leads me to believe that Google used the tool from the future just to make that tweet. Very cunning of them.

For a quick translation their message, have a look at the binary day tool!

[P.S. I’m hosting an alternative version at N&H which will handle international character sets: http://binary.noiseandheat.com/. Join the fun worldwide!]
